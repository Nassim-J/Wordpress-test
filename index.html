About us

  • Growing up in Mount Lebanon, our family developed a mindful attitude towards the country’s natural riches. In 2016, this compassion motivated all of us to go out of our ways, leave our office jobs, and launch our first family run store that became our brand “Orquidea Garden Center”

  • In the first year we established our nursery where we grew flowers, vegetables, herbs, and fruit trees, both local and exotic. Within two years our store transformed from a flower shop into an environmental experiment with a vision:

    “To provide ourselves and our community with the means to eat healthy food, breathe clean air, and bring greenery to our surrounding, both indoors and outdoors.”

We’ve established strong relationships with local artists who supply our store with various equipment.

We’ve also allocated a section to sell fresh locally grown organic produce in collaboration with local farmers.

Our family’s compassion towards nature has shaped our lifestyle and framed our day-to-day activities. Our customers are our friends, fellow environment enthusiasts who visit us to share their stories and strengthen their relationship with nature.

Nature is our Home, and Orquidea Garden Center feels like home.

The community’s welcoming and supporting energy to our journey has never ceased all along.

In return to this felt enthusiasm we’ve been organizing gardening workshops for kindergartens and schools; connecting agricultural specialists with local farmers seeking advice; helping families design; assisting business owners in decorating their offices; collaborating with local municipalities in decorating roundabouts; creating outstanding customized gifts and flower arrangements for all occasions.
